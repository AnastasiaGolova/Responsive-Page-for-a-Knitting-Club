About us

Have you ever heard about online coworkings? Of course, you have. We are an online co-knitting! Our goal - to create an international community of knitting-lovers. The knitting is your hobby or your job - it doesn't matter! You are welcome to our community. We have several options for knitting together, you can find them below. We also support crochet lovers.

Events
